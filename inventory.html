Phase 1 · Odoo Inventory

Never run out mid-season.

One stock system for the dive centre, bar, kitchen and retail shelf — tanks, beverages, F&B, dive gear and souvenirs all tracked in the right units — so nothing runs dry the weekend a full boat of divers rolls in.

Screenshot: Inventory replenishment dashboard for Casa Escondida — image or videoto be added
Flawless replenishment

Reorder before the shelf is bare.

Set a min-max on every SKU — 60 filled tanks, a case of San Miguel, a fresh batch of regulators — and Odoo raises the purchase order the moment stock dips below the line. Buy in the units your suppliers use (kegs, cartons, cases) and stock in the units your team counts on the floor. No more Sunday-morning scramble when the dive boats are already booked.

Screenshot: min-max reordering rules with auto purchase order — image or videoto be added
Offline barcode counts

Count stock even where the wifi doesn't reach.

Scan barcodes and QR codes with a phone or handheld across the bar, kitchen, dive store and retail corner — receiving deliveries, running cycle counts and moving stock between locations. Everything works fully offline down at the boat house or the tank room, then syncs the second you're back on signal. Fast, accurate, no clipboards.

From low stock to on order

Stockouts, handled before you notice.

Odoo watches every location around the clock and turns a dwindling shelf into a purchase order automatically — so the next full house arrives to full shelves.

All the features done right

Built for a resort with a dozen moving parts.

Multi-location

Separate stock for the dive store, bar, kitchen and retail shelf — with internal transfers between them, all under one roof.

Valuation methods

FIFO, average or standard cost so you always know the true cost of every keg, tank fill and retail item.

Screenshot: stock valuation report by location — image or videoto be added

Put-away strategies

Slow and fast movers, ABC analysis and cross-docking so hot-selling drinks and daily gear land where staff reach first.

Wave, cluster & batch picking

Group orders by area or by run so restocking the bar and pulling retail parcels happens in one efficient trip.

Traceability & reporting

Lots and serial numbers on tanks and gear, with real-time stock, move and activity reports whenever you need them.

Quality control

Quality checks during receipt and storage — so a bad delivery or a tank due for servicing never quietly slips onto the floor.

Phase 1 · Odoo Point of Sale (Restaurant)

A PoS made for the resort.

One counter that runs the restaurant, the bar and the beach deck — orders, kitchen tickets and payment on a single screen — and every peso can post straight to the guest's room folio, so the bill is settled once, at check-out.

Self-service

Self-ordering for the win.

Let guests take the wheel. A kiosk by the dive shop and a QR menu on every table let divers and families browse, customise and order at their own pace — while your team stays on the floor pouring drinks and clearing plates. Orders land at the counter the instant they're placed, so nothing gets missed after a long day on the boat.

Loyalty & rewards

Reward loyal guests.

Build that loyalty into the counter: hand out coupons for the next stay, run happy-hour promotions on the beach deck, top up eWallets for dive groups, and let regulars earn on every meal and cocktail — all tracked against their profile automatically.

All the features done right

Everything the counter needs, built in.

All devices welcome

Run it on a tablet at the bar, a desktop at reception or a phone on the deck — same counter, any screen.

Offline payments

Anilao's signal comes and goes. The counter keeps taking orders and payments offline, then syncs the moment it reconnects.

Multiple cashiers

Give each server their own login on a shared counter, so tips and takings are always attributed to the right person.

Integrated payments

Card, GCash and e-wallet run through a connected terminal — no re-keying totals, no mismatched tills at close.

Screenshot: connected payment terminal at the counter — image or videoto be added

Flexible invoicing

Issue a proper invoice on the spot for corporate dive trips and group bookings, straight from the same order.

Charge to room folio

Post any tab to the guest's room in one tap. Meals, cocktails and snacks roll into one clean bill at check-out.

Phase 1 · Odoo Sales

Quotations that convert.

TechNext configures Odoo Sales around the way Casa Escondida actually sells — dive packages, group stays and agent rates — so every enquiry from the dock or the inbox becomes a clean, branded quote in seconds.

Screenshot: main Odoo Sales quotation / sales-order screen — image or videoto be added
Quote templates & portal

From enquiry to signed order without the email tag.

Save your dive packages, room nights, gear rental and F&B as reusable templates, then send a branded quote with the right taxes and pricing already applied. Guests and dive-shop agents open a portal link to review, e-sign and pay their deposit — no back-and-forth threads, no lost bookings before the season fills up.

Screenshot: quotation template builder & guest/agent portal view — image or videoto be added
Pricelists

Walk-in, agent and seasonal rates — applied automatically.

Keep one catalogue, sell it three ways. Walk-in divers, contracted travel agents and peak-season stays each get the right price the moment they are added to a quote, so the front desk never has to remember who pays what. Update your high-season rates once and every new order follows the rule.

Loyalty & rewards

Reward loyal divers.

The divers who come back every season are your best revenue. Odoo Sales gives you the tools to keep them returning — coupons for the off-season, loyalty cards that stack across trips, gift cards for the dive shop and an eWallet balance guests can spend on their next stay.

All the features done right

Built for the way a dive resort takes bookings.

Deposits & flexible invoicing

Capture a down-payment to lock in group stays, then bill the balance with pro-forma, milestone or delivery invoices that flow straight into the guest folio.

Screenshot: down-payment deposit & invoice on a group booking — image or videoto be added

Online payment & e-signature

Divers confirm from anywhere — sign the quote and pay the deposit online, so a booking is locked before they land in Anilao.

Marketplace & OTA ready

Pull orders from your online and travel-agent channels into one place, so retail and package sales never need re-keying by hand.

Upselling built in

Offer nitrox, extra dives, gear rental or a spa add-on right on the quote and in the portal — extras guests can accept in one tap.

Reporting & forecasting

See sales by package, agent and season at a glance, with revenue projections that help you plan boats, staff and inventory ahead of peak.

Guest & agent portal

Every guest and travel agent has a self-service portal to track quotes, orders and dive vouchers — fewer emails, happier repeat divers.

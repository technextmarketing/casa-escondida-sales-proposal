Odoo Sales · set up by TechNext

Quotations that turn enquiries into booked dives.

TechNext sets up Odoo Sales around the way Casa Escondida sells — dive packages, room-and-board bundles, group trips and courses — with professional quotes, e-signature, online payment and automatic upsells, so an enquiry becomes a confirmed booking in a few clicks.

Orders, guest service and the shop — on one platform

Sales orders, front-desk service and your online booking shop all run in the same system, so nothing is re-keyed and every team sees the same numbers.

Quote templates that write themselves

Stop rebuilding the same quote. Save templates for your common offers — a 3-day / 6-dive package, a Discover Scuba course, a corporate group weekend — and Odoo applies the right products, pricing and taxes for each guest automatically. Reps spend their time selling, not formatting.

Close bookings faster with a guest portal

Let guests confirm on their own time. Each guest gets a self-service portal to view their quotation and booking, pay the deposit online, and e-sign the dive waiver and agreement — no back-and-forth emails, no printed forms at check-in.

Only chase the leads worth chasing

Enquiries come from everywhere — website, social, referrals, dive expos. Odoo captures them all in one place and its AI lead scoring surfaces the ones most likely to book, so your team follows up on the right divers first.

Pricing built for every kind of guest

Walk-in, resort guest, dive club or corporate group — set the right price for each without manual maths.

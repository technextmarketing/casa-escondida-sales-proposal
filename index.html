The rollout

A phased plan, built to reduce risk.

TechNext delivers Odoo in clear phases — the foundation first, then operations, with your online presence running in parallel. Here's the shape of it.

Phase 1 · Weeks 1–15

Foundation

Contacts, Inventory, Sales, POS (Restaurant), Studio, CRM, Accounting and a custom Hotel / PMS module — the one-folio backbone for everything else.

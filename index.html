Introduction

One resort, one system, one bill.

This proposal shows Casa Escondida running live on Odoo — already set up with your rooms, your guests and pesos on screen. A guest checks in, dives, eats, rents a camera and shops, and every peso lands on a single folio. Below is that system in action, module by module. The screens are real; the numbers are demo.

One guest, one folio

Everything a guest does, on one invoice.

Room nights, three dives, a rented camera, dinner on the beach deck and a rash guard from the shop all post to the same guest folio — audited into one clean invoice. At check-out there's a single bill to settle, and every line has already hit the right account.

Charge to room

Restaurant, bar & dive shop bill to the room.

Orders taken at the counter or the beach deck flow straight onto the guest's folio, so the whole stay adds up in one place. No handwritten chits, no reconciling five departments at the desk when it's time to leave.

Local & foreign guests

Bill in pesos or dollars — whoever's checking in.

Anilao draws divers from everywhere. Quote and settle a folio in ₱ for local guests and in $ for international ones, with the exchange handled for you, so the price a guest sees is always in the currency they expect.

Deposits & balances

Always know what's paid and what's due.

Take a deposit at booking and the balance shows clearly against the folio until it's settled. The front desk sees exactly who still owes what, so no one checks out with an open balance by accident.

Groups & dive clubs

Split the folio any way the group wants.

A dive club arrives on one booking but wants separate bills? Split the folio in a few clicks — shared costs on the master, extras on each guest — so group check-outs are quick and nobody argues over the bar tab.

Cancellations

Refunds handled cleanly, on the record.

Weather turns and a trip is called off — issue a refund against the folio in a controlled, auditable way. The books stay right and the guest gets a clear record, no manual adjustments in a spreadsheet.

Front desk

Check-out in a tap, straight from the dashboard.

See today's arrivals and departures at a glance and close a stay with a single check-out request — the folio finalises, the room frees up, and the books post themselves.

Phase 1 · Odoo Accounting

Accounting made effortless.

Every night at Casa Escondida the front desk, the dive shop, the bar and the retail counter all take money — and by morning it should already be in the books. With Odoo Accounting the POS, guest folios and dive charges post themselves to the right journals, so your ledger is current before the first boat leaves the dock.

One ledger, no re-keying

Everything posts itself to the right journal

A room night, a fun-dive package, a round of San Miguels at the bar, a mask sold off the rack — each one lands in its own journal the moment it happens. No exporting POS totals into a spreadsheet, no re-typing folio charges at month-end. The books simply keep pace with the resort, department by department.

Screenshot: journal entries auto-posted from POS, folio & dive charges — image or videoto be added
Reconcile in minutes

Smart bank reconciliation across banks & e-wallets

Guests pay by card, by bank transfer, by GCash and in cash — and every one of those flows needs to be matched. Odoo syncs your statements and auto-matches most transactions against the right invoices and payments, so reconciliation across all your accounts becomes a quick review instead of a two-day hunt.

Bills & expenses, handled

AI invoice capture and expenses from your phone

Snap or forward a supplier bill — for boat fuel, tank fills, fresh fish, linens — and AI reads the amounts and files it for you to validate. Out on a supply run in town? Staff photograph the receipt on the spot and it's captured automatically. Nobody sits on a pile of paper waiting for month-end.

Phase 1 · Odoo CRM & Contacts

Every enquiry, maximised.

A booking request from an OTA, a DM on your socials, a walk-in at the dive shop — capture them all in one pipeline, with a full guest record and dive-cert history behind every name, so nothing slips through between high season and low.

Contacts

One record for every guest.

Keep names, emails, home countries, agents and suppliers in one address book — with each diver's certification level, logged dives and past stays on file. When a returning guest emails again, the front desk and dive team already know their story before they reply.

Screenshot: guest contact record — dive certs & stay history — image or videoto be added
Unified inbox

Every conversation in one place.

Email, live chat, SMS and VoIP calls all land on the same enquiry card — no more hunting across inboxes, phones and OTA extranets. The full thread with each guest sits right on the pipeline, so whoever picks up the enquiry has the whole conversation in front of them.

Screenshot: enquiry card with email, chat, SMS & call log — image or videoto be added
Follow-ups & quotes

Follow up on time, quote in seconds.

Schedule the next call or reminder the moment an enquiry comes in, so no dive-package or group booking ever goes cold mid-season. When a guest is ready, turn the enquiry into a polished quotation straight from your catalogue — rooms, dives, gear and boat trips — and send it as a PDF without leaving the record.

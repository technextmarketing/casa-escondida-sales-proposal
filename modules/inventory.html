Odoo Inventory · set up by TechNext

A store-room that counts itself.

TechNext turns Odoo Inventory into a single source of truth for Casa Escondida's stock — the F&B pantry, the bar, dive consumables and the retail shop — so every sale draws down its items and the numbers on screen match the shelves.

Stock never runs dry mid-weekend. Set minimum and maximum levels and Odoo proposes — or automatically raises — the purchase order for tanks, food, fuel and bar supplies. It can even nudge the supplier to confirm the delivery a few days ahead.

Automate vendor reminders too!

Receive, check and put away — fast

Log deliveries against the order, run a quick quality check, and shelve stock with put-away rules that keep the fast-moving items — bottled water, snacks, o-rings — within easy reach. Less walking, fewer mistakes on a busy delivery morning.

Know where everything is

Track lots, batches and expiry dates in real time — food, first-aid and tank hydro-test dates — and look up any item in a second. Cycle counts keep the records honest without ever closing the store-room for a full stock take.

Pick the efficient way

Odoo supports several picking strategies, so gathering items for a dive trip, a beach event or a big group booking takes the shortest path.

Wave picking

Group tasks by area and regroup at one point — handy for a larger back store.

Cluster picking

Gather several orders in one trip to save time — ideal for small items.

Batch picking

Combine multiple orders into one pick and consolidate at the end.

Deliveries, sorted

When you move stock between the store-room, the dive shop and the kitchen — or send supplies out to a day-trip boat — Odoo handles the labels, quality checks and different pack sizes, from boxes to crates, in one place.

Built to cover the details.

Quick barcode scanning

Receive stock and run counts fast — scans work offline and read barcodes, QR and GS-1, even for items without a barcode.

Smart routes

Map how stock moves between store-room, dive shop, kitchen and bar, and let Odoo plan the transfers automatically.

Accurate valuation

Keep the value of the store-room correct in the books, with FIFO, average or standard costing.

Clear reservations

Stock earmarked for a booking, a course or a group trip is set aside, so it isn't accidentally used elsewhere.

Full traceability

Follow any lot from the supplier delivery to the plate or the tank it went into — live reports on stock and movements.

Phase 2 · Odoo Rental

Rent the gear. Track it.

Every wetsuit, BCD, regulator and underwater camera at Casa Escondida — booked online, priced by the dive, signed for at the shop and tracked from pickup to return. No more guessing what's out on the boats or chasing kit that never came back.

Screenshot: main Rental dashboard for the dive centre — image or videoto be added
Book online

Guests reserve gear before they arrive.

Divers pick their wetsuit size, BCD, regs and camera straight from your website and check out with the dates they need. Set per-dive, daily or weekly rates for each item and Odoo prices the reservation automatically — so the kit is set aside and paid for before their boat even leaves the jetty.

Screenshot: online gear booking with per-dive / daily / weekly pricing — image or videoto be added
Pickup & return

Always know what's out, due back and overdue.

One live board shows every piece of gear on loan: which diver has it, when it's due at the shop, and what's running late. Your dive staff check items out at pickup and back in at return in a couple of taps — so the store always knows exactly what's on the boats and what's ready for the next guest.

All the features done right

Everything the dive centre needs to rent with confidence.

Digital contracts

Rental agreements signed before any gear leaves the shop, so there's a clear record for every item on loan.

Unavailable days & padding

Block dates when pickup or return isn't possible, and add turnaround time between rentals so kit is dried, checked and ready.

Screenshot: blocking unavailable days & setting padding time between rentals — image or videoto be added

Late-return fees

Automatic delay charges apply when gear comes back after the agreed time — no awkward conversations, no lost revenue.

Integrated inventory

Rental stock lives in the same system as the rest of the resort, so every fin, tank and camera is counted in one place.

Maintenance tracking

Log servicing and inspections for regs, tanks and BCDs so safety-critical gear stays in top condition between guests.

Deposits

Take a deposit at pickup and settle it on return, protecting the shop against damaged or unreturned equipment.

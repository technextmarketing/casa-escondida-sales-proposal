Phase 2 · Odoo Maintenance

Plan maintenance, not emergencies.

A regulator that free-flows at 18 metres, a compressor that quits before the morning fill, a boat engine that stalls on the way to the site — at Casa Escondida these are guest-facing failures. Odoo Maintenance keeps every tank, reg, BCD, compressor and boat on a preventive schedule, so gear gets serviced on the calendar, not in the middle of a dive.

Preventive scheduling

Service the gear before the gear fails you.

Tell Odoo how often each item needs attention — tanks by hydro date, regulators by dive count, the compressor by running hours — and it raises the maintenance request for you ahead of time. It even computes MTBF and MTTR from your own history, so the schedule tightens around the equipment that actually gives you trouble. Your dive masters walk to the boat knowing every rig on it is in date.

Screenshot: preventive maintenance rule for a regulator set — frequency, computed MTBF/MTTR and next service date — image or videoto be added
Pre / post-dive checks & logs

Every rig, checked in and checked out.

Log the quick pre-dive check as gear leaves the shed and the post-dive check as it comes back — corrosion, o-rings, port pressure, a knock on a fin. Weekly recurring tasks keep the compressor, first-aid O2 and boats on a fixed rhythm. Nothing is remembered "in someone's head": every check is a dated record, building a full safety trail you can hand to a PADI auditor or an insurer without scrambling.

All the features done right

Everything you need to keep the dive centre running.

Kanban & calendar

Drag maintenance requests across stages and see every scheduled service on one calendar, so nothing overlaps a busy dive day.

Screenshot: Kanban board plus month calendar of equipment servicing — image or videoto be added

Performance KPIs

Computed stats and custom KPIs on failures, downtime and cost show which gear to retire and which to keep servicing.

Mobile access

Dive staff raise and update requests from a phone on the boat or the jetty — no waiting to get back to the shop laptop.

Trigger from the floor

Spot a cracked fin strap or a sticky inflator? Raise a request on the spot with a real-time alert to the maintenance team.

Equipment history

Every service, repair and check stays attached to the item, so each tank and reg carries its own full maintenance record.

App integration

Ties into Inventory and Rental so gear pulled for service drops out of the rental pool automatically — no double-booking.

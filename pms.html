Phase 1 · Odoo Hotel / PMS

Reservations & one guest folio.

From the first booking enquiry to the final sign-out, Casa Escondida runs on one screen. A live reservation calendar keeps every casita accounted for, and a single guest folio pulls together the room, the dive packages, the bar tab and dinner — so departure morning is a warm goodbye, not a paperwork scramble.

Reservation dashboard

See the whole week across every casita.

One calendar shows every room, every night — who is arriving, who is staying, who is checking out. Colour-coded availability means the front desk can promise a dive-weekend booking with confidence, and blocked-out dates and long-stay reservations are visible at a glance. No more double-booking a room the same weekend the boats are full.

Check-in & check-out

A smooth arrival, an easy goodbye.

Guided check-in captures the guest, assigns the casita and opens the folio in a few taps — so the welcome drink comes before the paperwork. At departure, the same flow closes the folio, settles the balance and frees the room for housekeeping. Fewer steps at the desk means more time looking after the guests actually standing in front of you.

All the features done right

Everything the front desk needs, in one place.

Availability at a glance

Live room availability across the calendar so peak dive weekends and long weekends never get overbooked.

Folio posts to journals

Every charge on the folio — room, dives, bar, dinner — posts to the correct accounting journal automatically, no re-keying.

Screenshot: folio charges posting to accounting journals — image or videoto be added

Group bookings

Dive clubs and family trips booked as one group, split across casitas, with folios that stay tidy and easy to settle.

Housekeeping status

Rooms flip to dirty at check-out and clean when they're ready, so the team knows exactly what's turnaround-ready.

Custom PMS for adoption

Shaped around how your front desk actually works, so the team takes to it quickly instead of fighting the software.

Guest history

Every returning diver's past stays, room preferences and spend on one profile — for a welcome that feels personal.

The business benefit

Why it matters for Casa Escondida.

  • One guest, one folio — a single clean bill at check-out
  • No overbooking, even on the busiest weekends
  • Faster check-in and check-out for a better guest experience
  • Every charge flows straight into the books

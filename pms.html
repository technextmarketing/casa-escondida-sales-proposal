One folio, from first enquiry to check-out.

Odoo has no off-the-shelf hotel app — so TechNext builds Casa Escondida a custom Property Management module on Odoo: a live reservations board, one consolidated guest folio, fast check-in/out and direct online booking, all wired into POS, Accounting and the dive centre.

Every room and boat seat, at a glance

A live availability board for the whole resort. See which rooms are booked, which dive slots are open and who arrives today — drag a reservation to move it, and the calendar updates everywhere. No more double-booked rooms on a full dive weekend.

Room, dives, dinner & gear — one bill

The heart of the module. Everything a guest spends — the room, three dives, a rented camera, dinner on the beach deck, a rash guard from the shop — posts to a single folio. At check-out there's one clean bill to settle, and every line has already hit the right account.

Check-in and check-out in a tap

Pull up the reservation, assign the room, register the guests and open the folio in seconds — and handle dive-club and corporate group bookings as one party with a shared master bill and individual extras.

Book direct, online

Guests book and pay a deposit straight from your website, and Odoo keeps availability in sync so the same room is never sold twice. TechNext wires in the channels Casa Escondida already uses.
